Process

Four steps.
Under 20 seconds.

The full screening workflow — designed to be used with minimal training by any community health worker in a remote clinic.

Step 01
Place device
Health worker places the microphone firmly on the patient's chest at the precordium.
Step 02
Record 15s
Arduino Nano captures 1,500 heart sound samples at 100 samples per second.
Step 03
AI analysis
Python extracts MFCC audio features and runs them through a Random Forest model trained on 3,240 recordings.
Step 04
Result
Probability scores appear instantly. CLEAR or REFER — with full confidence breakdown shown below.

Context

What is RHD?

Rheumatic Heart Disease is entirely preventable — but only if caught before the damage becomes irreversible.

45.5‰
Pacific prevalence
RHD prevalence in Tuvalu reaches 45.5 per 1,000. Nauru 36.6, Solomon Islands 24, Kiribati 20.6 — among the highest rates in the world.
$0.50
Cost to prevent
A single penicillin injection can prevent RHD entirely — but only if detected early. Most Fijian patients are diagnosed only when they need open-heart surgery.
46.3%
Heart failure in Fiji
In Fiji, RHD accounts for 46.3% of all heart failure admissions in people aged 0–40. 25.8% of known RHD patients are hospitalised for complications.
100×
Cost vs echocardiography
Echocardiography costs over $15,000. CardioScan targets under $150 — making screening viable for any remote Pacific clinic with no specialist required.
